Webmaster 2018

Theme: Artificial Intelligence

 

 

Technologies:

 

WordAI is dedicated to using the most advanced technology to ensure that your AI has all the capability to learn and perform your task at the highest level of efficiency. Some of our technology helps your AI learn at record-breaking rates, while some help make sure that your system can handle any processing requirement that you may face. Some of the technologies that we have implemented into every AI includes:

 

Machine Learning:

Machine learning is a computing discipline thats goal is to allow computers to “learn” based on collecting data and then use this “knowledge” to perform tasks autonomously without any human supervision. When you order an AI, machine learning is what we use to ensure that your machine takes in all the data provided and will produce the output that you need.

 

Backpropagation Convolutional Networks:

This type of network is used to send data through various layers and “learn” from it so the AI can respond to a certain scenario with accuracy. This is based on convolutional structure and calculus in order to find the optimal weightings of the system in order to keep the result error at a minimum. All products from WordAI make full use of the backpropagation to minimize the potential error in your AI. We are so confident in our algorithm that we are willing to ensure that your error will be under 1%, guaranteed.

 

Parallel Computing:

All of our AI will make use of parallel computing. Ran on graphical processing units, this will greatly improve the amount of operations per second that your system can run. This is based off running operations at the same time instead of the traditional sequential methods. The increased efficiency comes from running smaller operations simultaneously, or breaking down larger operations into smaller parts that are performed alongside each other. With our AI using this technology, the processing limitations are nearly non-existent.

 

Generative Adversarial Networks

When training AI, WordAI uses generative adversarial networks to ensure that the AI has an advanced understanding of the problem trying to be solved. This involves having the AI both create and solve problem scenarios. This can be both performed with a single system or two “competing” systems. This way allows your AI to make the max usage out of the data used.

 

Business Statement:

We can guarantee that we will utilize the most advanced and efficient technology to be sure that your AI will learn with record-high accuracy, process data with the topline hardware, and perform its task beyond your expectations.

 

 

 

Uses:

 

When it comes to using an AI, there are limitless possibilities for what could be done by your AI. Just like a human, AI is capable of learning so that it will be able to accomplish whatever tasks may be needed. Not only is AI capable of doing unskilled labor at speeds impossible by a human, AI can also has the capability to perform skilled labor just as well, if not better, than a human. WordAI has a goal to try to provide you with whatever will satisfy your labor requirements. If you are in the research industry, you may have problems with analyzing terabytes of data manually and detecting all the trends that may be found. AI can do this in a matter of minutes and find correlations that no human could ever see. In education, AI could be used to help identify problems going on in the classroom and bring it up to a teacher. Just by finding trends in grades this could flag where students may be having issues. AI’s biggest application is likely in business. Businesses could streamline their manufacturing through the use of AI. Instead of having people supervise manufacturing, the entire process would be entirely automated. Even when performing international business deals, AI would be of great use. Natural language processing is already one of the biggest uses of AI, when companies pick up usage for their business, their costs on translators will be greatly reduced. Even if you are not some big business of government, it may come in hand for your own personal use. When its time for household tasks you may dread doing, its no worry if you have an AI that could do a better job with no effort put in from you. So if you ever have a task that needs to be performed but can’t be afforded or labor can’t be found, just let WordAI know. We will find a way that you can utilize AI to get your job done.

 

 Business statement:

Regardless of what you need in your personal life or your industry, we are here to help. Our hardware will be personalized to fit your task. As long as there is data available on your task, your AI will be trained using the most effective neural network methods available. Any needs that you may have, we can train a custom AI to perform the task just as well as any human.

 

 

Costs:

 

The pricing model of an AI is variable based on what it is needed to perform. Some uses such as natural language detection can be utilized for a very affordable cost, while other uses such as predicting cancer growth may have a cost increase related to the advanced processing performed. The primary cost will be the cost of the physical processing hardware and storage. The price of processing has been steadily decreasing every year. Today, 9.3 million instructions per second only cost one dollar. The price of storage is also decreasing rapidly. Today, one gigabyte only costs about two cents. The secondary cost is the cost of the training data. This price is all based on the type of data needed. Data such as search trends can be acquired at a rate of dollars for thousands of entries. Other data such as research results can run at a much higher rate based on the intensity required in collecting the data. If you have access to your own data, the only cost will be hardware.

 

Business statement:

There is no standard price for an AI, but with information about what you need to be done, we can provide you with a quote on how much that will cost. We will cover it all from finding a dataset to work with and what processing power you will need. We guarantee we can get you the most affordable option to get your AI up and running.

 

 

Safety:

 

In modern day society, there is a common misconception that AI is something that should be feared. Many people believe that AI is an idea that is malicious by nature. WordAI proudly stands by AI being a tool purely for benefit. Many people fear that their jobs, privacy, and well-being are at risk by the usage of AI. This is all just a common misconception. Job security should not be a problem, since while a few jobs consisting of tedious labor may be lost, the new technology will usher in new fields that required skilled workers to keep up with the technology. While some people may lose jobs, in general the net gain will benefit the whole workforce. Our users also do not have to worry about their privacy being at risk. Right now, a major concern is that big companies are using and abusing the data they collect on you. WordAI will maintain a position where your data will not be disclosed to any third party or used for any purpose that you do not agree to. While the media may portray AI to be some dangerous idea that will end humanity, we contest that AI is anything but. In the end, AI is still just a computer, and computers do what they are told. As long as we do not explicitly program malicious activity (which we won’t), there will be no malicious activity. When considering using our service, it is important to keep these details in mind. WordAI will guarantee that there will be no harm done to you, short term or long term, as a result of using our various AI products.
