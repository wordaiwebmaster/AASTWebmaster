VEX

 

Vex Robotics allows students the ability to develop engineering, communication, and project management skills.

 

This competition helps to incorporate STEM by allowing students the opportunity to design, build, and even program a robot.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Robotics Club

 

Robotics club is an innovative club in which students obtain the opportunity to use their engineering skills to create robots that attain the capability of handling various scenarios. These scenarios maintain strict rules, a time limit, and specific limited resources.

 

Students apply their STEM skills and work as a team to build their industrial-sized robot to compete in the FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics Competition, in which the robot must complete three tasks. These tasks include that the robot must shoot balls, carry gears on and off of a platform, and climb a rope.
