Service Projects

Our Community Service Projects:

 

 

Soles4Souls

Soles4Souls is a very important service project our chapter has participated in for the past three years. We have a drive to collect as many gently used shoes as possible throughout our school. This organization then gives these shoes to people in need around the world. This year we collected over 300 pairs of shoes for the drive.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

American Cancer Society

 Our chapter got very involved with the American Cancer Society this year. We tried to raise as much funds and awareness as possible for the organization. We participated in many fundraising activities. We created a Relay for Life Team and we also sold ACS Relay for Life Feet that students could buy for $1, write their name on, and hang on the cafeteria wall. We used our resources to raise a total of over $5000 so far.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

United Against Cancer Benefit

 Our chapter got involved with a major service project put on by the students leaders of the entire school. AAST TSA members helped host the United Against Cancer benefit that had a dinner and silent auction to raise money for Children’s Cancer Partners of the Carolinas. AAST TSA donated various silent auction items and provided volunteers. Through these efforts, $15,288 was raised for children battling cancer in our local community.
