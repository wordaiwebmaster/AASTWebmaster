Computer Science

The Computer Science major is offered for students who desire to go into software development or are interested in computer design.

 

This major is taught by Mr. Blake Vaught, who has a Masters in Instructional Technology.

Entertainment Technology

AAST also provides students with the opportunity to join the Entertainment Technology major, in which students who are keen on becoming broadcast journalists or going into television and video production are able to get an inside look at this career.

 

This major is taught by Mr. Tracy Johnson, who is a former Calvin Gilmore Productions employee.

 

Pre-Engineering

The Pre-engineering major is taught by Mr. Bucky Sellers, an engineer himself.

He designed the major to allow students the exploration of various areas of engineering in which they are interested.

Web & Digital Communications

Students who wish to pursue photo editing, web page developing, or photography, among others, are permitted to get a head start on the career and prepare themselves for a future in digital arts in the DC major.

This major is taught by Ms. DeSimone, who has a degree is graphic design from USC.
