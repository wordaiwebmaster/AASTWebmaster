TSA & STEM

 

AAST TSA:

 

Our TSA chapter first started during the 2011-2012 school year, and ever since then our chapter has continued to grow and become better each year. We have over seventy students this year. Our chapter has become very involved in both our community and school by creating new service activities and promoting and advocating for TSA and STEM.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

AAST TSA Activities:

 

Elementary School Tours

We  welcomed elementary school students and gave them tours around AAST to get them interested in STEM majors and TSA. This event allowed elementary school students to gain exposure to the engineering process and high technology that is utilized in our school.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

TSA Meetings

Our TSA chapter meets bi-weekly to discuss items of business and collaborate as a chapter. This is a time when individual can work on their projects and ask advisors and officers questions.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Fundraisers

Every year, we have multiple fundraising events to raise as much money as possible for our chapter and to help fund students who qualify for the national conference. We had a fundraising night at Ben and Jerry’s on this day of Leap week. We also sold snacks at the First Lego League Competitions at our school throughout the year.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Open Houses

Our chapter officers attended open houses to recruit new members for TSA.
